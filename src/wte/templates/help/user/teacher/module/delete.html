Delete a ModuleÂ¶

To delete a module, hover your mouse over the icon and then select the Delete menu item. As deleting the module cannot be undone, you are then asked to confirm that you want to delete the module.

Warning

Deleting a module will also delete all its parts, pages, any assets, templates, and any learner work. This action cannot be undone.
